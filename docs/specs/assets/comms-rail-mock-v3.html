The Comms Rail v3 — your toolbar, your sessions

Mock, not live code. The four toolbar icons are the control surface: click one → that category's last session of conversations lands on the rail (tabs + whatever popups you had open). Click it again — or click a sibling — and the rail sweeps clean. One category at a time, nothing at login until you reach. Tabs pop their conversation Messenger-style, right above themselves, several at once. Each session ends with the dashed ALL tab — the un-ended-conversations menu (FIRST tab on the left) with Open / End per row.
