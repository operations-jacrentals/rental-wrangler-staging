What changed from v1

  • Account is now top-of-card & collapsed by default — one-line summary (company · phone · email + type). Expand for the compact fields.
  • Rental Protection is its own row, a real toggle, with a one-line explainer when on.
  • Rows are one line — the membership status overtakes the title (Membership Pending, Renewal Failed); a plain active member just reads its type. Card-on-file is colored text, no box.
  • Collapsed summary chip is overtaken by the worst agreement status when present (here Renewal Failed, red) — otherwise it shows the account type.
  • NOT SIGNED · NO SELFIE · NO CARD are grouped red flags on the row (same treatment).
  • Footer is compact: Cancel · Save · Start Membership. The +Agreement/Card add-row hides while you're inside a new agreement.
